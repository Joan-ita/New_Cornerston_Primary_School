New Cornerstone Primary School

We believe that every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered and nurtured. Founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and creativity, our school provides a safe and stimulating environment where learners grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that empowers every child to become confident, responsible, and compassionate members of society.

Our Vision

To be a leading centre of learning that inspires lifelong curiosity, creativity, and character development in every learner.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching, learning, and discipline.

Respect

We encourage kindness, empathy, and appreciation for diversity.

Integrity

We promote honesty, responsibility, and strong moral character.
